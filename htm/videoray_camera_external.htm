VideoRay External Camera

A second external camera can be mounted on the Pro 4 and directed laterally, toward the rear or vertically up or down.

The VideoRay Pro 4 supports to video circuits that can be switched between the main camera and the second external camera. When one camera is active, the other camera cannot be viewed.

Two simultaneous video signals can be supported if the APIC (Auxiliary Pair of Independent Conductors) in the tether is used for the second video signal.

This equipment is optional and may not be included in all VideoRay Pro 4 configurations.
