VideoRay External Camera Operating Tips

Hardware Set Up

The external camera can be mounted in the sockets in the top of the float block. The cable should be connected to the ROV accessory port.

The ROV accessory port should be sealed with either a dummy plug or the manipulator or cutter.

Operation

The selection of which camera is active (main or external) is accomplished by pressing the camera select button on the hand controller or the camera select button on the camera instrument.

This equipment is optional and may not be included in all VideoRay Pro 4 configurations.
